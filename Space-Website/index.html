1 2 3

THE TERMINOLOGY…Kepler Mission

Launched in 2009, the Kepler space telescope revolutionized exoplanet science by discovering over 2,600 confirmed exoplanets, including Earth-sized planets in habitable zones.

THE TERMINOLOGY…Transiting Exoplanet Survey Satellite

Launched in 2018, TESS searches for exoplanets around the brightest stars near Earth, focusing on smaller planets in the habitable zones of nearby stars.

THE TERMINOLOGY…James Webb Space Telescope

Launched in 2021, JWST is equipped to study exoplanet atmospheres in unprecedented detail, searching for biomarkers like oxygen and water vapor.
